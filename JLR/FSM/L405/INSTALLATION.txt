INSTALLATION

This version adds global search across all folders and PDFs.

1. Extract this ZIP.
2. Copy/merge the contents over your existing L405 FSM folder.
3. Allow it to overwrite generated HTML files.
4. Keep your PDFs and folders exactly where they are.

Notes:
- Search works from any generated page.
- Search results include folders and PDFs from the whole FSM.
- Links are relative/self-referencing only, not C:\ paths.
- The homepage only shows the supplement PDFs as direct root files.
