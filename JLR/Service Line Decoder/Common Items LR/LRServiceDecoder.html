Jaguar Land Rover Service Lines

Examples of service lines for Land Rover

  • 1. LR166061 - Screen Wash Land Rover (Used for all services except EasyAuto123)
  • 2. "Renew Dynamic Response Fluid Reservoir" - 2x STC50519 + 1x LR072550
  • 3. "Renew Brake Fluid" - 2x LR052653 (SVR cars differ)
  • 4. "Renew Interior Air Filter" - 1x Pollen Filter (Can be found in ECat by searching "Pollen")
  • 5. "Renew Engine Oil and Filter" - 1x Oil filter + 1x Oil Drain/Sump Plug
  • 6. "Replace the Smart Key battery" - 2x /YWK + 2x Battery Warning Tags
  • 7. "Replace the Fuel Filter" - 1x Fuel Filter (Can be found in ECat by searching "Fuel Filter")
  • 8. "Replace the air cleaner element" - 1x Air filter or 2x Air Filter for twin turbo models
  • 9. "Replace spark plugs" - ?x Glow/Spark Plugs (?= the amount of cylinders the car is)
  • 10. "Replace all flexible brake hoses" - 4x flexible brake hose to caliper for each corner of the car (Each corner is different part no.)
  • 11. "Renew transfer box oil" - 2x LR048842 + 1L of fluid per speed in Transfer Case (1-2L)
  • 12. "Renew locking differential oil" - 2x 1L of rear axle/rear differential oil
  • 13. "Renew automatic transmission oil" - 9x 1L of transmission oil
  • 14. "Renew power transfer unit oil" - 2x 1L of rear drive oil/PTU fluid
