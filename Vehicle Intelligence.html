Vehicle Intelligence

One entry point for VIN identification, vehicle specifications, genuine accessories, fluids, service information, technical bulletins, workshop manuals and common parts. Unknown VINs must use a manual vehicle selector; the system does not guess.

Honda VIN intelligence is active. Other Australian-market brand data packs will connect to this shared interface as they are verified.

Shared intelligence modules

These modules form the standard record for every brand.

Vehicle identityYear, model, body, variant, chassis, engine and transmission.
SpecificationskW, Nm, kerb weight, kW/tonne, drivetrain and 0–100 km/h.
AccessoriesApplicable orderable part numbers, notes, FRT, kits and source records.
Service & fluidsSpecifications, capacities, intervals and common service parts.
Technical resourcesFSMs, TSBs, recalls, known issues and related documents.
