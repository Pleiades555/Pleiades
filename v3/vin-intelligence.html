How to use these references

Use a matching VIN or chassis number to reach the correct EPC vehicle family, then verify the target vehicle’s build date, model code, powertrain, body and fitted options before ordering parts. Unknown or unresolved details remain clearly marked.

Public Australian listing VINs retain their source links. User-confirmed imported factory VINs, surrogate VINs and Japanese chassis numbers are preserved as research identifiers and do not automatically prove Australian delivery, engine, transmission, DCCD or parts fitment.
